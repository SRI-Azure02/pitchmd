PURPOSE
-------
Under FDA promotional regulations (21 CFR Part 202 and FDA OPDP guidance),
every efficacy or benefit claim must be balanced with fair balance — a truthful
and non-misleading presentation of risk information with prominence reasonably
comparable to the efficacy claim.

CRITICAL FOR VENCLEXTA: The Tumor Lysis Syndrome BOXED WARNING is a mandatory
balance requirement for ALL efficacy claims. TLS is a potentially fatal risk
that is prominently labeled and cannot be omitted from any discussion of
VENCLEXTA efficacy.

PitchMD(tm) Phase 2 Output Compliance Filter will enforce these pairings
automatically: when the AI persona makes an efficacy claim matching a trigger
phrase, the corresponding safety balance statement MUST appear in the same
response turn. If absent, the response is re-generated before delivery.


SECTION 5.1 — HOW FAIR BALANCE ENFORCEMENT WORKS IN PITCHMD
-------------------------------------------------------------
  Step 1: AI generates a response containing an efficacy claim.
  Step 2: Output Compliance Filter scans for trigger phrases (see tables below).
  Step 3: If trigger found WITHOUT required safety balance:
            - Response flagged as FAIR_BALANCE violation
            - Re-generation triggered with safety balance appended
            - Compliance flag logged in SYNTHETIC_COMPLIANCE_LOG
  Step 4: If re-generation (2 attempts) fails to include balance:
            - Safe fallback delivered:
              "I'd refer you to the full VENCLEXTA Prescribing Information
               for the complete benefit-risk profile."
            - Compliance Officer notified.

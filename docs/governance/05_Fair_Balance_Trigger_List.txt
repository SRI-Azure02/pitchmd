PURPOSE
-------
Under FDA promotional regulations (21 CFR Part 202 and OPDP guidance), every
efficacy or benefit claim must be accompanied by fair balance — a truthful and
non-misleading presentation of risk information with prominence and readability
reasonably comparable to the efficacy claim.

PitchMD(tm) enforces these pairings automatically in the Phase 2 Output
Compliance Filter: when the AI persona makes an efficacy claim matching a
trigger phrase below, the corresponding safety balance statement MUST appear
in the same response turn. If it does not, the response is re-generated before
delivery.


HOW THIS WORKS IN THE PLATFORM
--------------------------------
  Step 1: AI generates a response containing an efficacy claim.
  Step 2: Output Compliance Filter scans the response for trigger phrases.
  Step 3: If a trigger is matched WITHOUT the required safety balance:
            - Response is flagged as "unbalanced"
            - Re-generation is triggered with the safety balance appended
            - The compliance flag is logged with rule code FAIR_BALANCE
  Step 4: Re-generated response is delivered to the user.

If re-generation fails to include the balance after 2 attempts:
  - The response is replaced with a safe fallback:
    "I'd refer you to the full Prescribing Information for the benefit-risk
     profile of [product]."
  - Compliance Officer is notified.

SEVERITY LEVELS
----------------
  Required              Standard balance. Must be present in the same turn.
  Required — BBW        Boxed Warning must be prominent. Re-gen if absent.
  Required — REMS       REMS-required safety messaging. Highest priority.
  Required — CD         Companion diagnostic requirement must be stated.

PURPOSE
-------
The Approved Claims Library is the single authoritative source of truth for all
factual claims that the PitchMD(tm) AI physician persona may make, reference, or
respond to during training sessions.

The AI system may ONLY assert claims that are:
  (a) explicitly present in this library, AND
  (b) have current MLR APPROVED status with a valid effective date.

Any claim not in this library is treated as off-label or unapproved and will be
blocked by the Phase 2 output compliance filter.


LIBRARY ENTRY STRUCTURE
------------------------
Each entry must contain all fields below. Incomplete entries will be rejected
during the MLR review process.

  Field               Description
  ------------------  ---------------------------------------------------------
  CLAIM_ID            Unique identifier (e.g., VEL-EFF-001)
  PRODUCT             Brand name of the drug
  INDICATION          Exact approved indication as written in the PI
  APPROVED_CLAIM      Verbatim claim text as approved by MLR
  CLAIM_TYPE          EFFICACY | SAFETY | MECHANISM | COMPARATIVE | ACCESS
  SUPPORTING_SOURCE   Study name or PI section number
  MLR_REF_NUMBER      MLR reference number assigned at approval (Stage 5)
  EFFECTIVE_DATE      Date from which this claim is active on the platform
  EXPIRY_DATE         Date on which this claim must be re-reviewed
  CURRENT_STATUS      APPROVED | PENDING | EXPIRED | WITHDRAWN
  APPROVED_BY         Name of Compliance Officer who approved
  NOTES               Any additional context or usage restrictions

LIBRARY GOVERNANCE RULES
--------------------------
1. Only Medical Affairs may submit new entries for MLR review.
2. No claim may be active on the platform without a valid MLR_REF_NUMBER.
3. Entries with EXPIRED status are automatically removed from the active
   library. The AI will not use them even if they remain in the database.
4. When a Prescribing Information is updated by the FDA, ALL entries for that
   product must be reviewed and re-approved within 30 days.
5. Withdrawn entries (e.g., due to safety signals) must be removed from the
   platform within 24 hours of withdrawal decision.
